This Privacy Statement explains how Furniro collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you interact with our website, purchase our products, or contact our support team. It also explains what privacy rights you have and how to exercise them.

A Collection, Use & Disclosure

The Categories of Personal Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information about you:

Personal Details

When you register or checkout, we collect contact information such as your name, email address, phone number, and postal address for order fulfilment and account management.

Payment Details

We collect payment method information, billing address, and transaction history to process your purchases securely. We do not store raw card numbers — payments are handled by certified payment processors.

Purchase & Order Information

We collect details of items you purchase, items in your cart, order history, shipping preferences, and returns information to fulfil your orders and improve our service.

Usage & Device Information

We automatically collect information about how you browse our website, including pages visited, time on site, search queries, device type, browser, and IP address.

Communications

If you contact our support team, respond to surveys, or leave reviews, we collect the contents of those communications to improve our service and assist you effectively.

How We Use Your Personal Information

  • To process and fulfil orders, including shipping, tracking, and returns.
  • To manage your account and provide customer support.
  • To send transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping updates) and, with your consent, marketing emails.
  • To personalise your browsing experience and product recommendations.
  • To improve our website, products, and services through analytics.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.
  • To respond to your enquiries and resolve disputes.

Who We Disclose Personal Information To

We disclose personal information to the following categories of third parties:

  • Delivery Partners — Logistics and courier companies who ship your orders.
  • Payment Processors — Certified payment service providers who handle transaction security.
  • Analytics Providers — Services that help us understand website usage patterns.
  • Marketing Platforms — Email and advertising platforms used only with your consent.
  • Legal Authorities — When required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety.

B Your Rights & Controls

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have some or all of the following rights regarding your personal information:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Right to Deletion

Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Right to Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold.

Right to Object

Object to our processing of your data for direct marketing or profiling.

Right to Restrict

Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@furniro.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Communication & Marketing Preferences

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any of our emails or by updating your account preferences. We will continue to send transactional communications (order confirmations, shipping updates) regardless.

Cookie Choices

You can manage your cookie preferences through our cookie consent banner or through your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website, such as remembering your cart items.

C Access to Account

You can access and update most of your personal information by logging into your Furniro account. From your account dashboard you can view order history, update your address book, change your password, and manage communication preferences.

If you need assistance accessing or deleting your account, please contact our support team. Note that we may retain certain information for legal and business purposes even after account deletion.

D Cookies & Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyse traffic, and personalise content. These include:

  • Essential Cookies — Required for core functionality such as maintaining your shopping cart and login session.
  • Analytics Cookies — Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it (e.g. Google Analytics).
  • Marketing Cookies — Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness.
  • Preference Cookies — Remember your settings and preferences for a more personalised experience.

You may withdraw consent for non-essential cookies at any time via our cookie settings panel.

E Other Important Disclosures

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, secure payment processing, and access controls to protect your personal information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Retention of Personal Information

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Order records are typically retained for 7 years for accounting purposes.

Other Websites & Platforms

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

Minors

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by email or by posting a notice on our website. Your continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
