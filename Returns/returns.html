How to Initiate a Return

Contact Us

Email our support team at returns@furniro.com or call us within 30 days of receiving your order. Include your order number and reason for return.

Get Approval

Our team will review your request within 24–48 hours and send you a Return Merchandise Authorisation (RMA) number and prepaid shipping label.

Pack & Ship

Securely package the item in its original packaging where possible. Attach the prepaid label and drop it off at any authorised shipping location.

Receive Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

Return Eligibility

Eligible for Return
Items in original, unused condition with all tags attached
Products returned within 30 days of delivery
Items with manufacturing defects or damage upon arrival
Wrong item received
Items in original packaging with all accessories included
Sale items (store credit issued, no cash refund)
Not Eligible for Return
Items returned after 30 days of delivery
Custom-made or personalised furniture orders
Items showing signs of use, wear, or assembly
Items with missing parts, accessories, or packaging
Damage caused by improper use or assembly
Gift cards and digital vouchers

Refund Timeline

Day 0

Return Request Submitted

You contact our team with your order number and reason for return. You'll receive an acknowledgement email immediately.

Day 1–2

Approval & RMA Issued

Our team reviews your request and emails your RMA number along with a prepaid return shipping label.

Day 3–7

Item Shipped & In Transit

You drop off the package. Estimated transit time to our warehouse is 2–4 business days depending on your location.

Day 8–9

Item Received & Inspected

Our quality team inspects the returned item. You'll receive an email confirming receipt and inspection outcome.

Day 10–14

Refund Processed

Your refund is issued to your original payment method. Bank processing time may add 2–5 additional business days.
